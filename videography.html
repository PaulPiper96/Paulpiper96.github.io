This is passion project i made with friends in summer of 2021. We all love film and the craft behind it so we decided to start a project of our own in our spare time. The special requirement was that we were very limited in terms of budget and time so we had to think of a script for one location with one actor and limited ressources (only filiming with 2 lenses and one sony 7m3). I do love the constraints for these kind of projects because the limitation makes you really double down on the creative decessions possible

Sony 7m3, Davinci Resolve, Cubase

Task:

short social media style travel film

Filmmaking is all in all telling stories. Video-Content for social media is not filmmaking. But I love doing that. I feel like these travel shorts are to filmmaking what quicksketches are for fine art portaits. You really have to know your basics to be able to frame shots and portray a mood without anything but a camera. I got really influenced by Peter McKinnon and people like him for these kind of projects.

Sony 7c, Davinci Resolve
