Pauls Gambit

Idea:

Short Movie with limited ressources as time, set, actos, equipment
‚Äç

This is a passion project my friends and I embarked on during the summer of 2021. We all share a deep love for film and the artistry that goes into it, so we decided to create something unique in our spare time. Our main challenge was the severe budget and time constraints. We had to devise a script set in a single location with just one actor and limited resources, including using only two lenses and a Sony a7 III camera. I found these constraints to be inspiring for this type of project because they compelled us to focus on creative decision-making and really make the most of what we had available.

Sony 7m3, Davinci Resolve, Cubase

Travel

Task:

short social media style travel film

Filmmaking, at its core, is the art of storytelling. While video content for social media might not fit the traditional definition of filmmaking, I find immense enjoyment in creating it. I view these travel shorts as the equivalent of quick sketches in fine art portraiture. To effectively convey a mood and frame shots with nothing but a camera, a deep understanding of the basics is essential. I've drawn inspiration from creators like Peter McKinnon and others who excel in these kinds of projects. Their work has had a significant influence on my own approach.

Sony 7c, Davinci Resolve
