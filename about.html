I've previously worked as a freelance web designer and wedding photographer, both of which I enjoyed. However, I believe that working within a professional team could provide significant benefits. While university provided me with a broad and abstract education, collaborating with others can take one's work to the next level. I hope this portfolio offers a glimpse into who I am and what I do.
